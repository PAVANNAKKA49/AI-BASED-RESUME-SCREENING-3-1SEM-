Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of the AI Resume Screening Tool?

Our tool is designed to streamline the recruitment process by using AI to screen resumes efficiently, helping recruiters find suitable candidates faster.

How does the screening process work?

The tool analyzes resumes based on specific criteria set by employers, such as skills, experience, and qualifications, and ranks candidates accordingly.

Can I customize the screening criteria?

Yes, you can customize the criteria to suit your hiring needs and preferences, making the screening process more effective for your organization.
